Hello There!

  • I am so excited you're here! I am Taraneh but I often go by Tara for short.
  • I am from Oklahoma and am very proud to be half Native American from the Kiowa Tribe. There aren't a lot of Natives in tech and one of my biggest goals is to get more exposure to the natives of all tribes so a career in tech can become possible!
  • I was lucky to have gotten the exposure to the programming world in college. I took several beginning classes in Java, CSS/HTML, C++, C#, Visual Basic, operating systems and networking.
  • I had always dreamed of becoming a software engineer and time kept passing while I stayed unhappy in a job in retail for years.
  • Finally I decided to just teach myself! I went online and studied at Codecademy, Khan Academy and FreeCodeCamp.
  • I was excited again!! I reached out to fellow coders instagram to see their projects and how they were. I just wanted to be in that world. Through my Instagram searching, it wasn't long before I found out about Hackbright.
  • WOW! An engineering school for women! I put everything I had into getting admitted and was THRILLED when I was accepted!
  • I quit both of my jobs, emptied my savings, threw everything I owed into a storage unit and flew to San Francisco to learn everything I could about software engineering.
  • I love everything about my new life. I am constantly being challenged and I enjoy the opportunity.
  • Being a software engineer is pretty awesome
